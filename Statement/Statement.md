**The Problem Statement :**

In the traditional banking world, when a person applies for a loan, a bank officer has to check their documents manually. This old-fashioned way of working has several big problems:

**1.It is Very Slow:** It can take days or even weeks for a human to review all the details, which frustrates the customers.

**2.Human Mistakes and Bias:** Different bank officers might have different opinions. One officer might approve a loan while another rejects the same application, making the process unfair.

**3.Financial Risk:** If a human makes a mistake and approves a loan for someone who cannot pay it back, the bank loses a lot of money
